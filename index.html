Overview - JJM Medical College

JJMMC is one of the premier private medical colleges in Karnataka, established under the flag of Bapuji educational institution in the year 1965 and is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Bangalore. The College has more than 5 decades of experience in the medical field and aims to provide leadership and excellence in medical education, research, and services. The College has the best state-of-the-art medical facilities among all Medical colleges in India. It has a team of experienced and highly qualified faculty members who are dedicated to imparting quality education and training to students.

Accreditations & Approvals:

  • Approved by the Government of Karnataka
  • Approved by the MCI
  • Approved by National Medical Commission (NMC)
  • Approved by Indian Nursing Council (INC)

Reasons to study at JJMMC

  • Experienced Faculty: The college has a group of well-qualified and experienced in their respective fields. Their experience and guidance impart new insight to the students in their medical educational careers.
  • State-of-the-Art Infrastructure: JJMMC has standard state-of-the-art infrastructure including well-equipped laboratories, Hospitals for children and women, central skill labs, a massive library with large collections of books, and many more.
  • Research Opportunities: It encourages research among students and provides opportunities to engage in research projects under the guidance of faculty members.
  • Focus on Holistic Development: In addition to academic excellence, JJMMC also emphasizes the overall development of the students, It offers various extracurricular activities, sports facilities, and cultural events to promote physical fitness and creativity.
  • Integrated & Experiential Learning: It aims to provide an opportunity to work alongside medical professionals to promote practical learning, and to expand your horizons and insights in the learning process.
  • Incubation Center: It provides a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs and innovators to develop their ideas into viable projects or startups in the healthcare domain.

Eligibility Criteria

Under-Graduate

M.B.B.S
  • Students must be 17 years old by December 31st of the admission year.
  • Students must secure a minimum of 50% in PCB. In the case of SC/ST students, 40 % and 45% for OBC.
  • For MBBS, Appearing in the National Eligibility and Entrance Examination (NEET-UG) is mandatory. (Government, Deemed & Private Colleges)

Post-Graduate

MD/MS
  • Candidates have to be Qualified in the PG entrance Test Conducted by the National Board of Examination, New Delhi.
  • Candidates must pass the leading course to the award of an MBBS Degree in colleges recognized by MCI and affiliated with any University established by law in India.

Campus Life @ JJMMC

Campus Infrastructure:

The College provides cutting-edge state-of-the-art infrastructure to enable effective learning. There are modern classrooms, labs, libraries, and research facilities on the college site.

Library:

The college's library is well-stocked with a large selection of books, periodicals, reference materials, and online resources related to medicine.

Hostel Facilities:

Male and female students are housed in separate dormitories at the college. Basic amenities including beds, study tables, and communal spaces for leisure activities are provided in the hostels.

Sports and Fitness Facilities:

The college promotes sports and physical fitness among students. It has well-equipped sports facilities like cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, badminton, and indoor games.

Advanced Learning Centre:

The Advanced Learning Centre focuses on training and workshops where students are taught Basic & Advanced Surgical Skills with practical knowledge.
